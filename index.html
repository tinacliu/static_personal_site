about

work / finance consulting

While working in the structured finance desk at a leading hedge fund, I worked on a wide range of investment opportunities in the asset-backed space, with a key focus in the consumer credit space. I was solely responsible for potential investments that I covered, including all stages of the investment cycle from initial analysis to execution and completion of the deals. Then I moved to the sell-side providing financing against a wide range of fixed income instruments including RMBS, CMBS, CLO, ABS, NPLs, Corporates, both liquid and illiquid. I now work in this space as a contract consultant.

/ Examples of Past Consulting Projects _

> Working in a team of three, using data tapes to building cashflow models to predict future cash flow for ten NPL portfolios bought by client. For portfolios where actual data is available, overlay actual data with model predicted cashflow, to produce detailed MI for each portfolio.

> Working with a consumer finance start-up in the U.K. at the early stage to advise on their capital funding options. Including working out the economics of each option and adjusting the business model to take into account the most likely capital funding route and associated costs.
